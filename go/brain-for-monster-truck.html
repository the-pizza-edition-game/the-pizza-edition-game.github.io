About Brain for Monster Truck

Brain for Monster Truck mixes drawing and driving. You sketch a road segment, drive a little, and extend the line as needed to bring your truck to the flag without losing the crate in the bed. Stars sit on awkward paths, so smooth slopes and low angles matter more than speed. It’s often better to build short ramps, move, erase, and redraw than to commit to one steep climb. When you have to drop down, land flat and give the cargo time to settle before continuing. There’s no timer pushing you, and progress saves level by level, so patient routes beat risky ones. After a few stages the basics click: draw gently, avoid bumps, protect the crate, and collect all three stars on a clean run.
