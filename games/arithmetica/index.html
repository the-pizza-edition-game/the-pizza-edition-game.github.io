About Arithmetica

Arithmetica is a oneminute sprint of mental math. Equations appear quickly and you tap the correct answer; each fast, correct response adds a sliver of time, and misses waste precious seconds. Its simple to start and ramps up as your pace increases. Played daily, its a tidy way to sharpen arithmetic in short sessions. The loop is honestspeed comes from accuracyand the timer makes even basic problems feel engaging when youre chasing a personal best.
